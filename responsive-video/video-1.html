Bears. Here They Are

About this video

This video was recorded in 1999 at Oswald's Bear Ranch in Newberry, Michigan. A young Danny Inman was bored out of his mind after being dragged there from a vacation in Petoskey - a full 4.5 hours away. This were the only two active bears in the whole place. Danny has never recovered from the boredom.
