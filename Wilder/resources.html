Where to watch

Wilder's films are scattered to the winds of licensing agreements and streaming rights, so you have to get creative to see them all.

JustWatch

One of the best tools for finding where a film is currently streaming. This link has all of Billy Wilder films. More even than I covered here.

The Criterion Channel

The best known streaming service for classic and world cinema. Currently streaming The Apartment. There are also interviews and extras featuring Wilder.

HBO Max

HBO is now owned by Paramount, which is featured heavily in Sunset Blvd. The billioniare failson who controls this platform does not allow to connect so specific items on the service...so this comes with a big grain of salt.

The Internet Archive

If you want to stick it to the man, you can watch Sunset Blvd for free through the Internet Archive. If you can stand the Spanish subtitles, have at it.

Conversations with Wilder

Book; Cameron Crowe

Director Cameron Crowe sat down with Wilder for a series of interviews covering his entire career.

On Sunset Boulevard

Book; Ed Sikov

The most cited biography of Billy Wilder. Sikov traces Wilder's life from Vienna to Hollywood in exhaustive detail. Its the full story.

Roger Ebert Film Criticism

The late, great Roger Ebert wrote reviews of Wilder's work as well as interviewed the director under the title of his "Great Movies" series.

Criterion Collection

The Criterion Collection (used to) publish essays alongside their releases. Several Wider titles are included in the collection.
